## Core Features:

- Sound Association Activities: Interactive activities that associate sounds with images and movements to stimulate speech development.
- Guided Tracing Exercises: Progressive tracing exercises focusing on lines, curves, and letterforms to develop fine motor skills essential for writing.
- Visual Learning Modules: Utilizing visual aids to reinforce connections between speech, sounds and written forms, aiding cognitive organization.
- Progress Tracking: Monitor and track progress to tailor the learning experience and make use of gamification
- Content personalization: Generate customized content tailored to a child's progress level and unique needs using provided data. AI acts as a tool, determining optimal activity levels.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#89CFF0) to evoke a sense of calm and trust, important for creating a safe learning environment for children with TEA/TDAH.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E5F5FF) for a gentle, non-intrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: Warm yellow (#FFDB58) to highlight interactive elements and provide positive reinforcement.
- Headline font: 'Poppins' sans-serif for headlines to ensure clarity, but with a geometric touch to suggest contemporaneity.
- Body Font: 'PT Sans' to enhance readability without causing visual overload.
- Simple, clear icons to visually represent different activities and concepts.
- Consistent layout and predictable interactions for enhanced learning predictability and safety.
- Subtle, gentle animations to indicate progress and provide feedback.
